## Chosen first-wave features
- Gambler Bloom plant
- Splitter Zombie
- Blood Moon event

## Reason for selection
These three additions create visible identity fast:
- plant-side volatility
- enemy-side surprise pressure
- event-side atmosphere and tempo shift

They are expressive enough to change the game feel without requiring a large architectural rewrite first.
